Featured Projects

Here are some of my recent projects that I've done in partnership with local nonprofits. I'm very grateful to the nonprofit admins and project teams I worked with that made these experiences possible!

  • Dreams For Schools


    A web based application that streamlines the process of assigning instructors to teach Dreams For Schools' K-12 programs.
  • OC Habitats


    A desktop and mobile compatible web application for managing monitor logs in collaboration with OC Habitats, a nonprofit dedicated to species conservation in Orange County.
  • Child Creativity Lab


    A video platform in partnership with Child Creativity Lab to teach children ages 6-11 about science, technology, engineering, art, and mathematics (STEAM).

About Me

Hi! I'm Lancy, a recent graduate from UC Irvine with a B.S. in Software Engineering. I see UX as a bridge between tech and people. With my computer science background, I believe that having a technical lens allows me to create data-driven designs that lead to meaningful human connections.

In my free time, I also enjoy video/board games, romantic comedies, skincare, and trying new food places around the Bay Area.
